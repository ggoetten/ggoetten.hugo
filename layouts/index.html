Here you may find important information about teaching class I am lecturing and my personal thoughts about research academia. I love research - discover new techniques and teaching students find the love in what they are currently studying.
If you are a researcher interested in working with me, please message me at my email and we could probably work with something together. For my teaching experience I am still an adept learning the ropes of fulfilling the student gaps in gathering an excited interest in knowledge and the epistemology. I tend to use many TED talks for helping me in lecture which help broad my field of view; although, I do love to find new channels and softwares to help students understand difficult concepts of the theory aspects. With no further delay, welcome and I hope you enjoy your stay.
